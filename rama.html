About Sri Rama Prana Prathista

Sri Rama Prana Prathista is a sacred event that marks the establishment of divine energy in the idol of Lord Rama. It is a significant ceremony celebrated with great devotion and enthusiasm.

How to Reach Ayodhya

Ayodhya is well-connected by road and rail. The nearest major railway station is Ayodhya Junction. If you are traveling by air, the nearest airport is Lucknow International Airport, and from there, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to Ayodhya.
